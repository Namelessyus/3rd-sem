E-commerce

E-commerce, or electronic commerce, involves buying and selling goods/services, or transmitting funds/data, over an electronic network, primarily the internet, and can be categorized as business-to-business (B2B), business-to-consumer (B2C), consumer-to-consumer, or consumer-to-business
